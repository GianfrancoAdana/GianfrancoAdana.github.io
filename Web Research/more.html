About UCOTSC

United Calinan Operators Transport Service Cooperative, located in Davao City, is a cooperative that provides transportation services to its members. Established in the year 8000, the cooperative is situated at the corner of Ramon Magsaysay Street and Villafuerte Street. It offers a wide range of services, from chartered services to regular public transport, to ensure that its members can get around safely and conveniently. The cooperative is well-known for its commitment to providing quality services and for its dedication to the local community. Through its services, it helps the local economy and creates jobs for the people of Davao City.
