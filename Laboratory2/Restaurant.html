MANG INASAL OFFERS JUICY IHAW-SARAP FOR TAKEOUT OR DELIVERY


READ MORE ABOUT US!

          Mang Inasal provides the proper mix of traditional Filipino food and the dine-in concept. It quickly won the hearts of its customers with its distinctively Pinoy stamp-grilling and special marinade made out of local secret spices. Famous for its native-style and "Nuot-Sarap" Chicken Inasal with its pioneering "unlimited rice," it has satisfied its customers' cravings with its distinctively Pinoy special marinade. The restaurant was an instant hit, with over thirty locations opened in just three years. Mang Inasal first launched for franchising in 2005, confident in the business's durability and brand recall, focusing his efforts in Visayas> and Mindanao, where Inasal is most popular. Luzon had franchise proposals soon after.
  • Pecho Large


             It is a chicken skewered and grilled the Mang Inasal way and served with a cup of rice. All rice dishes include a cup of rice and the option of unlimited rice servings. There's an option to order three rice bowls for takeout or delivery. A spicy version is available.


  • Pinoy Halo-Halo


             Mang Inasal's Pinoy Halo-Halo is a popular Filipino dessert made with a rich concoction of numerous ingredients such as nata de coco, ube, leche flan, pinipig rice, and evaporated milk, all topped with crushed ice and the option of ube or vanilla ice cream. Mang Inasal has elevated their game to new heights, and they've been dubbed the Creamiest Pinoy Halo-Halo of all time.
